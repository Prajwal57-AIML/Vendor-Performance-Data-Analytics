# 🚀 Vendor Performance Optimization | Data Analytics Case Study

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a **real-world end-to-end Data Analytics case study** focused on analyzing vendor performance to drive **data-driven business decisions**.

Using **SQL, Python, and Power BI**, the project identifies high-performing vendors, detects inefficiencies, and provides actionable insights to **optimize profitability and operational efficiency**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Organizations often struggle with:
- Identifying **top-performing vs underperforming vendors**
- Managing **unsold inventory (dead capital)**
- Improving **profit margins and operational efficiency**

👉 This project solves these challenges through **data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and reporting**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **SQL** → Data extraction & cleaning  
- **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)** → EDA & statistical analysis  
- **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard & business insights

## 🔍 Key Steps Performed

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation (SQL)
- Extracted and transformed raw vendor data  
- Resolved **95% data quality issues**  
- Ensured consistency for accurate analysis  

---

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (Python)
- Analyzed sales, profit, and vendor contribution  
- Identified patterns and trends  
- Visualized key metrics using **Matplotlib & Seaborn**  

---

### 3️⃣ Statistical Analysis
- Performed **Hypothesis Testing**  
- Calculated **95% Confidence Intervals**  
- Validated reliability of vendor performance insights  

---

### 4️⃣ Dashboard Development (Power BI)
Built an **interactive dashboard** with:
- 💰 Total Sales: **441.41M**  
- 💸 Total Purchase: **307.34M**  
- 📈 Gross Profit: **134.07M**  
- 📊 Profit Margin: **38.7%**  
- ⚠️ Unsold Capital: **2.71M**  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### ✅ Top Performing Vendors
- Major contributors like **Diageo, Pernod Ricard, Bacardi**  
- Top 10 vendors contribute **~65.7% of total sales**  

### ⚠️ Low Performing Vendors
- Identified vendors with low sales & poor margins  
- Opportunity to **reduce losses and optimize supply chain**  

### 🏷️ Brand-Level Insights
- Top brands drive majority of revenue  
- Clear visibility into **high vs low demand products**
